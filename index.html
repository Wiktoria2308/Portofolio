After this front-end course with HTML, CSS and JavaScript, I felt that programming is something for me and I knew that I had to continue my education. I've started to learn Swedish and then made Programmering 1 at Komvux with A degree, Software Development Academy at Lund University and programming course at Lexicon. Along the way, I did many online courses and worked on many individual and group projects. I really would like to finally begin my career by getting a software developer job and by showing my skills and involvement.
